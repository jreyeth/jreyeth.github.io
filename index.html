Cool. What kind of art do you make?

My art is kind of like ASMR for your eyes.

I grew up and live in New England and take inspiration from the world around me. (Who doesn't?) I really dig rocks and clouds and root vegetables and moving bodies. My art incorporates natural shapes and textures and colors and movement.

I hope you feel curious and calm when you look at my art. If do, please let me know. And if you don't, please let me know what you do feel!

So, what's your story?

I'm a guy living in a mountain valley in northern New England. When I was younger I studied math at Harvard, then computer science at UMass Boston, and then systems biology at Harvard again.

These days my non-artist time is spent building software that forecasts the weather. I like spontaneous dance parties, and my favorite color is orange. All of these things make it into my art one way or another.
